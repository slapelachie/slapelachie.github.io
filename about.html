About Me

Hello! My name is Lachlan Slape. I am a programmer, GNU/Linux enthusiast and a full time nerd. I have an interest in reading, photography, music and pretty much anything that would keep me entertained. As a programmer, I follow many philosophies including: KISS (Keep it simple stupid) and the Unix philosophy of do one thing and do it right. As a lot of the things I create are scripts, my main programming language is Python. However I do mess around with source code programmed in C for my terminal emulator and window manager.

Outside of programming, I have a huge passion for sciences (mainly physics), mathematics, reading books and listening to music. I guess my interests in physics and mathematics probably stems from my love in programming, with how I love to learn how things work. My taste in music is extremely diverse, with interests from Rock to Classical to J-Pop, depending on what mood I am in.
